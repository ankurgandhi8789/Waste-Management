About E-Waste

E-waste, or electronic waste, refers to discarded electronic devices like phones, computers, and appliances. It is a growing environmental concern, as these devices often contain hazardous materials.

Proper management of e-waste ensures valuable materials are recycled, reducing the need for mining and lowering the environmental impact.

Recycling Process

1. Collection

Gathering discarded electronic items from consumers and businesses.

2. Sorting

Separating items into categories like plastics, metals, and usable parts.

3. Dismantling

Breaking down devices into components for further processing.

4. Recycling

Extracting valuable materials like gold, copper, and aluminum for reuse.
